Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an Expensio account, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and password. As you use the service, we also store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — so that we can display your spending history back to you.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide to operate your account, let you log in securely, and show you summaries and breakdowns of your own spending. We do not use your financial data to serve ads, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in a database associated with your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Expensio may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting and font services) to operate the app. These providers may process limited technical data, such as IP addresses, solely to deliver their service. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
